Interests

After my days work and before offshore calls begins at night, i try to carve out time for myself and my family. With two kids, filling up the time is not that difficult at all. Between helping the Middle schooler with her homework and playing with the Elementary age girl, i reduce any stress and rejuvenate myself. Depending on the season, i try to indulge myself in some gardening in our backyard and do some photography.

Apart from this, in order to stay fit i bike around my neighbourhood or sometimes bike at the localassets Don Edwards Wildlife Refuge. Watching all those beautiful birds provides a context to who we share the Earth with and our responsibility to protect the habitat for all living things.
